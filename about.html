About Us

Chrishano Burns (CEO)

I decided to start a car dealership because of my lifelong passion for automobiles and a deep-seated desire to provide people with high-quality vehicles and exceptional customer service. Throughout my life, I've always been fascinated by cars and the automotive industry, and I saw an opportunity to turn that passion into a thriving business. Moreover, I recognized a gap in the local market for a dealership that prioritizes transparency, honesty, and integrity in all its dealings with customers. I wanted to create a place where buyers could find the right vehicle to suit their needs and budget, while also having a trusted partner for their automotive journey. This venture allows me to blend my enthusiasm for cars with a commitment to delivering an unparalleled car-buying experience to the community.
